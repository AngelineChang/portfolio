Angeline Yuchi Chang

User Experience Designer


Hi, I’m Angeline Yuchi Chang (張育綺), currently a product designer at ASUS Intelligent Cloud Services (AICS) based in Taipei, Taiwan.

In my professional career, I have worked on organization-wise design system, large-scale enterprise products in web and mobile, and AI system. I streamlined healthcare professionals’ interconnected workflows and supported their clinical decisions through researching and designing intuitive enterprise AI software experience. I also helped fellow designers and engineers to design and build cohesive product experience faster through the creation, management, and evolvement of the organization’s design system.

My interest in user experience design grows from my fascination with human judgement and decision-making process as well as my zeal for crafting experiences. I’m passionate about designing products that augment human abilities with emerging technologies to unleash the potential of human-computer collaboration.

Outside of work, I enjoy sharing knowledge by facilitating design thinking workshops and mentoring aspiring designers, fostering the growth of design communities through organizing events and lectures, and supporting social impact initiatives by mentoring underrepresented groups in STEM and human rights projects. When I’m not immersed in design, I enjoy experimenting with matcha and coffee beverages, training at the gym for my next marathon, and finding balance through yoga and meditation.
